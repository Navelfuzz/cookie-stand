About Us:

We started in this small post-card town in the Pacific Northwest

called Seattle. Salmon are a integral part of our food culture.

But we asked ourselves if we're making smoked salmon for dinner

or canning it for snacks later... What are we doing about dessert?

We decided the only thing that could properly pair with a meal of Coho

were Salmon Cookies!
